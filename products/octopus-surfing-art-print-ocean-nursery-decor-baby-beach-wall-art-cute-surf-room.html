Frequently asked questions

What file formats will I receive?
You'll receive high-resolution JPG and PDF files (300 DPI) ready to print at home or at any print shop. No physical item will be shipped — this is a digital download you can access immediately after purchase.
What sizes are included?
Multiple print sizes are bundled into a single purchase: 5x7, 8x10, 11x14, 16x20, 18x24, and 24x36 inches, plus international A-series (A5, A4, A3) for standard frames outside the US. Print whichever size fits your frame.
Can I print this at home?
Yes — the files are optimized for home printing on heavyweight matte or semi-gloss photo paper (200 gsm or above). For larger sizes (16x20 and up) we recommend a local print shop or an online service like Shutterfly, FedEx Office, or Mpix for best color accuracy.
Can I print more than one copy?
Absolutely. Once you download the files they're yours for personal use — print as many copies as you like, for every room in your home, or to give as gifts.
