Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

RetroSwell ("we", "us", "our") operates an online store selling printable digital wall art through Etsy and maintains a product catalog website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly through this website. Our catalog site does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools. When you purchase from our Etsy shop, your transaction information is handled by Etsy under their privacy policy.

Social Media

We maintain accounts on social media platforms including Pinterest and TikTok to showcase our art. When you interact with our social media content, those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We do not collect or store any personal data from social media interactions.

Third-Party Services

Our products are sold through Etsy (etsy.com). Purchases, payments, and customer data are managed by Etsy under their privacy policy. We receive only the information necessary to fulfill digital download orders.

Data Security

We do not store personal customer data on our own servers. All transaction data is managed by Etsy's secure platform.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Etsy shop.
